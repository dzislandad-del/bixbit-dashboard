**Status: NOT FOUND** — HTTP 404 at https://bixbit.io/llms.txt

This is a **P1 gap**. The llms.txt standard (llmstxt.org) provides LLMs with a structured map of the site's most important content, context about the company, and pointers to key pages. | Criterion | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Direct answer in first paragraph | YES | "reduces power consumption and increases performance up to 160Th/s" [VERIFY] |

### Key citability gaps across firmware pages:

1. **No FAQPage schema** — FAQ content exists on S19 and likely other firmware pages, but no JSON-LD markup means Google cannot surface FAQ rich results and AI models cannot cleanly extract Q&A pairs.
2. **No Product schema** — missing name, description, compatibility, offers.
3. **Missing meta descriptions** — ALL 24 crawled pages have empty metaDesc. AI Overviews and search snippets fall back to body text, reducing control over how the brand is summarized.
4. **No efficiency table** — J/TH comparisons vs stock firmware are the #1 B2B decision signal. Absent on all pages. Competitors (Braiins, Luxor) publish these prominently.
5. **No Organization schema on homepage** — missing foundingDate, numberOfEmployees, areaServed, sameAs (social profiles).

## 7. Priority Findings

### P0 — No items. AI crawlers are not blocked.

### P1 — High impact, implement within 30 days

1. **Missing llms.txt** — Draft above is ready to deploy at bixbit.io/llms.txt
2. **Zero meta descriptions across all 24 pages** — Empty metaDesc means AI Overviews and LLMs pull uncontrolled snippets; fill with 150-character factual summaries per page
3. **Missing FAQPage JSON-LD** — FAQ content exists on firmware pages but has no schema; add FAQPage markup to all firmware landing pages immediately

### P2 — Medium impact, implement within 60 days

4. **No Organization schema on homepage** — Add JSON-LD with name, url, description, foundingDate, sameAs (LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter/X)
5. **No Product/SoftwareApplication schema on firmware pages** — Add schema with name, operatingSystem, applicationCategory, featureList, offers (devFee)
6. **Absent from third-party "best firmware" guides** — Pursue editorial inclusion in Hashrate Index, D-Central, OBM.io, and EMCD comparison articles; these are the AI citation sources

### P3 — Lower priority / longer lead time

7. **No efficiency comparison tables** — Add per-model J/TH tables (stock vs BiXBiT) to firmware pages; this is the primary B2B decision signal and the most-cited content format in competitor pages [VERIFY numbers before publishing]
8. **No llms-full.txt** — After llms.txt is deployed, add llms-full.txt with full technical specs, compatibility matrices, and FAQ content for deep LLM retrieval
9. **"/download/" blocks changelog/release notes** — If firmware changelogs live at /download/, move or mirror them to an accessible path so AI can index version history
