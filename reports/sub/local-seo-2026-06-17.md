## 3. Why Classical Local SEO Does Not Apply

1. **No verified BiXBiT street address is publicly disclosed** for any location.
2. **Product is digital-first**: firmware is downloaded, AMS is cloud SaaS, cooling
   hardware is shipped globally — no walk-in customer interaction at a physical location.
3. **ICP is global B2B**: buyers are institutional mining operators across 16+ countries,
   not local searchers in a single city/region.
4. **Google Business Profile requires a verifiable physical address** where staff are
   present. Using a partner address (Big Star Mining) would violate GBP policy and
   risk suspension.

## 4. Applicable Alternatives (Recommended)

### P0 — Organization schema (immediate, no address required)
Add `Organization` schema to bixbit.io/en with:
- `name`, `url`, `logo`, `contactPoint` (email + phone per region)
- `areaServed` listing target markets (US, CA, KZ, PY, AE, LATAM)
- `sameAs` linking to verified social profiles

### P1 — Global Presence content page (trust signal + SEO)
Create `/en/global-presence` (currently 404) with:
- Named regions served (North America, Europe, South Asia, LATAM, MENA)
- Partner/reseller network if BiXBiT authorizes them publicly
- No fake addresses; confirm what can be disclosed legally
- Signals international authority to both users and crawlers

### P1 — B2B directory citations (brand mentions without GBP)
List BiXBiT on directories that do not require a physical address:
- Clutch.co (software/tech provider profile)
- G2 (mining software category)
- Crunchbase (company profile)
- F6S, AngelList, LinkedIn Company Page
- Mining-specific directories: Hashrateindex vendor pages, Asicminervalue

### P2 — Localized landing pages (geo-targeted content, not GBP)
Create market-specific content pages for priority regions:
- `/en/mining-firmware-usa` — targeting US industrial miners
- `/en/mining-cooling-kazakhstan` — targeting KZ market
These can rank in regional SERPs without GBP listings.

### P2 — Poland entity NAP (conditional)
If BiXBiT decides to publish a full street address for the Poland HQ:
- Google Business Profile registration becomes viable
- NAP should be consistent across site, GBP, Clutch, LinkedIn, WHOIS

- Do NOT list fictional phone numbers or addresses in any directory
